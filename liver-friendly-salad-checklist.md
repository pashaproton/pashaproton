# Liver-friendly Salad Checklist

## 🥬 Base (Choose 1-2)
- Spinach
- Arugula
- Kale
- Mixed greens (no iceberg - low in nutrients)
## 🥕 Veggies (Pick 3-4)
- Beets (raw or cooked) – excellent for liver detox
- Carrots (shredded) – rich in beta-carotene
- Cucumber – hydrating
- Red or green cabbage – helps detox
- Bell peppers – high in vitamin C
- Broccoli or cauliflower (raw or steamed) – cruciferous veggies help the liver
- Tomatoes – antioxidant-rich (but moderate if sensitive)
## 🥑 Healty Fats (Choose 1)
- Avocado (¼–½ piece)
- Olive oil (for dressing)
- A few olives (unsalted or low-sodium)
## 🐟 Protein (Choose 1, grilled or steamed only):
- Salmon
- Grilled chicken breast
- Boiled egg
- Tuna (in water, low sodium)
- Turkey breast
## 🌱 Extras (Choose 1-2)
- Pumpkin or sunflower seeds (small handful)
- Chia or flax seeds
- Fresh herbs: parsley, cilantro, dill
## 🍋 Dressing
- Olive oil + lemon juice
- Balsamic or apple cider vinegar (unsweetened)

`❗ Avoid creamy dressings, ranch, or anything with sugar or seed oils.`
## 🚫 Avoid
- Croutons
- Cheese (esp. processed or salty ones)
- Sugary dried fruits (like cranberries with added sugar)
- Ranch, Caesar, Thousand Island dressings
- Processed meats (ham, bacon, pepperoni)
